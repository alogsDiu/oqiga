Who We Are

At Oqiga, we are passionate about bringing people together. Founded in 2024, we are a dedicated team committed to helping you discover local gatherings such as parties, tea parties, reading clubs, and more. Whether paid or free, we make it easy for you to find events that match your interests.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people through shared experiences and foster a sense of community. We strive to create a platform where everyone can find events that resonate with them, making it easier to meet new people and engage in activities they love.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the power of community and aim to strengthen the bonds between people through shared experiences.
  • Accessibility: Our platform is designed to be easy to use, ensuring that everyone can find and attend gatherings that interest them.
  • Inclusivity: We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome and valued.
  • Fun: Life is better when you're having fun. We want to make it easy for you to find enjoyable events and activities.

Our Story

Oqiga started with a simple idea: to make it easier for people to find local gatherings and connect with others who share their interests. From small beginnings, we've grown into a platform that offers a wide range of events, from casual meetups to organized activities. Our journey has been driven by our passion for bringing people together and creating memorable experiences.
